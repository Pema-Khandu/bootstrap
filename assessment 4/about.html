Nothing is softer or more flexible than water, yet nothing can resist it.

Water scarcity in Africa is predicted to reach dangerously levels by 2025. It is estimated that about two-third of the world's population may suffer from fresh water shortage by 2025. The main causes of water scarcity in Africa are physical and economic scarcity, rapid population growth, and climate change.
